07 · Go to market

Build. Prove. Sell.

The goal is not a giant launch. The goal is undeniable evidence that strangers understand the product and will pay for it.

01

Validate

Recruit 10–20 beta users, watch them use the demo, identify confusion, and test willingness to pay.

02

Sell

Launch a focused website, Lemon Squeezy checkout, founding pricing, and a lower-friction starter offer.

03

Grow

Use short demos, creator affiliates, customer stories, email education, and future AI subscriptions.

Proof milestone

Ten people who do not personally know the founder pay at least $79 for the product or founding edition.
