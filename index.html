Vision & Mission

The permanent reason the company exists.

Vision

Become the clearest and most trusted command center for people managing modern, complicated financial lives.

Mission

Give households a beautiful, private, and intelligent way to understand their money, prepare for change, and make better decisions without juggling disconnected apps and spreadsheets.

Product principle

We are not selling another budget tracker. We are building a financial operating system that turns scattered information into clarity, priorities, and action.

Ideal Customer

People whose financial lives have outgrown ordinary budgeting apps.

Primary persona: The Global Household

  • Age 35–55 with household income generally above $120,000.
  • Multiple accounts, investments, reserves, and tax obligations.
  • May relocate internationally, work remotely, or manage more than one currency.
  • Values polished software, privacy, organization, and decision support.

FIRE households, freelancers, veterans, relocating families, investors, and financially organized households that need more than transaction categorization.

Build Checklist

Definition of Done

  • No personal names or balances.
  • No production database access.
  • No credentials in browser payloads.
  • Reset works reliably.
  • Mobile is usable.
  • AI reads only fictional snapshots.
  • Beta users understand the value.

Launch Plan

The shortest responsible path to a first paying customer.

Validate

  • Recruit 10–20 beta testers.
  • Observe first-use behavior.
  • Identify confusion and objections.
  • Test willingness to pay.

Sell

  • Own marketing website.
  • Lemon Squeezy checkout.
  • Simplified Etsy edition.
  • Founding-customer pricing.

Grow

  • Short product demos.
  • Creator affiliates.
  • Email education sequence.
  • AI subscription upsell.

Validation goal

Prove that 10 people who do not personally know the founder will pay at least $79 for the product or a founding edition.

Operating Playbook

How the founder, repository, and background agents work together.

Weekly rhythm

  • PM agent proposes priorities.
  • Founder approves scope.
  • Work happens on branches.
  • QA & Privacy gates changes.
  • Only the founder merges and deploys.

Decision filter

Every proposed feature must improve conversion, onboarding, retention, trust, or revenue. Otherwise, it waits.
